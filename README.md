# EyesL - EyesLove (Not a brand name just to visualize)
This project was inspired by a inversed renderer which used uncaliberated photometric stereo and a custom wrapped SLAM engine
The goal is to make a tool which can help small store owners , brands and companies who dont have access to Quality Cameras and Photogrammetry rigs , we want to give a bundled app which can help them click pics which feels easy but outputs will be of higher quality.
I moved from measuring physical correctness to perceiveable quality which helped me reduce complexity and "TECHNICAL OVERHEADS"
I will share outputs of validation in folder "assets" to map the workflow and expand the outputs from clothing to shoes to other accessories as well. 
Not competing with big brands value proposition is not "BETTER THAN DSLRs OR AI MODELS" but trying to do something they cant.
